Origin of the Golden Kings' Oud

Golden Kings’ Oud is a fresh and ambitious cologne brand that officially launched on the 8th of August 2025. Founded by Mvuyisi Sokabo, a man known for his deep, refined understanding of fragrance and luxury. This brand is built on a passion for scent that goes above and beyond just smelling good. Mvuyisi’s vision was to create a cologne that speaks to identity, confidence, and timeless elegance—blending tradition with the modern sophisticated man. The brand is made for individuals who value class, confidence, and authenticity.

Our Mission:

To craft premium, oud-based fragrances that empower individuals to express their inner royalty with confidence and style.

Our Vision:

To become a globally recognized cologne brand.

Goals and Objectives:

  • Establish a strong brand identity rooted in quality, elegance, and cultural depth.
  • Expand into local and international markets, starting with South Africa and moving into the broader African and global fragrance scenes.
  • Innovate with scent combinations, blending oud with other notes like amber, citrus, and spice to create unique signature blends.
  • Build a loyal customer base through excellent service, storytelling, and community engagement.
  • Collaborate with influencers and retailers to grow brand visibility and credibility.
